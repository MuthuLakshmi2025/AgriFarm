What is Organic Farming?

Organic farming is a method of agriculture that avoids harmful chemicals and uses natural ways to improve soil fertility and crop growth. It maintains ecological balance and protects human health.

‚úÖ Organic Farming Tips

  • Use compost, cow dung, and green manure to improve soil nutrients.
  • Grow companion plants like marigold to control pests naturally.
  • Rotate crops yearly to protect soil and avoid diseases.
  • Harvest rainwater and use drip irrigation for efficiency.
  • Avoid GM seeds and choose local varieties for better yield.
